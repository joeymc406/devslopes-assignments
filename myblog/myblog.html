Why I like guns.

Early years with firearms

Growing up in Montana hunting is an extremely popular sport and pasttime that a great majority of families in our communities participate in. With it being so prevalent so is the use of firearms in the scope of hunting. I grew up learning hunting and firearm safety and used rifles most of my life for the purpose of hunting and also target shooting for fun. Growing up around them I have a great respect for them and understand them.

Career involiving firearms

Years later at the age of 23 I started a career in law enforcement as a police officer in which I was required to carry a Glock 22 for personal defense and defense of others in different situations. While I was required to carry a firearm I was also required to be trained with it and be proficient with it in a safe manner. During this time I began collecting them and enjoyed shooting them more for fun. I later purchased more pistols and AR-15 in that I use now for fun and for protections of myself and my family.

Constitutional right to bear arms

As a former Police Officer I understand the views on firearms and how people feel about them but I also understand that it is a fundamental right to have the capability, ability and means to protect yourself from others that wish to do you harm. The Constitution was created and within it the 2nd Amendement was created because our forefathers understood this necessity even with the limited technology of the time.
